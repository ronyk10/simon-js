Objective: The player must move all the crates or objects in the level to their target locations to complete the level.

Moves: The player can move the main character (usually represented by a character or an object) to push the crates in four directions (up, down, left, right). Crates can only be pushed if the space in front of them is clear.

Obstacles: The player must avoid obstacles such as walls or other objects that can block the movements of the crates or the main character.

Target locations: The crates must be moved to specific target locations on the game board. Once all the crates are correctly placed on the target locations, the level is considered successful.
